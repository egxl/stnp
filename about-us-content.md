## The Origin Story

### Label
Our Story

### Title
Building Trust Through Legal Excellence

### Content
Soaloan Tua Nababan & Partners (STNP) was founded in 2018 by **Oswald Anggi Soaloan, S.H.** — originally operating under the name *Soaloan and Partners* — with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between international-caliber legal services and deeply rooted Indonesian expertise.

Prior to founding the firm, Mr. Oswald built a distinguished career in business law across the mining, plantation, property, banking, finance, and energy sectors. His experience representing major Indonesian corporations laid the foundation for STNP's client-first philosophy.

### The 2021 Expansion
After three years of steady growth, STNP expanded its leadership in 2021:

- **Gideon Putra Tua Sitorus, S.H., M.H.** joined as Partner — bringing extensive experience in corporate transactions, commercial litigation, and plantation law.
- **Daniel Perdana Saoloan Nababan, S.H., M.H.** joined as Partner — a specialist in bankruptcy and insolvency proceedings for international and Indonesian clients.

All three founding partners are alumni of **Gadjah Mada University (UGM)**, one of Indonesia's most prestigious institutions. Their shared academic background and combined decades of experience created the foundation for a firm capable of handling complex, high-stakes legal matters.
