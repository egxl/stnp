# PRODUCT.md

## Brand Identity
**Soaloan Tua Nababan & Partners (STNP)** is a Jakarta-based boutique law firm specializing in high-stakes bankruptcy, commercial litigation, and corporate finance.

## Register
**brand**

## Core Principles
1. **Uncompromising Authority**: The firm handles matters that "define your business." The design must feel stable, heavy, and undeniable.
2. **Jakarta Excellence**: Acknowledging the firm's deep roots in the Indonesian legal landscape while maintaining international standards.
3. **Strategic Discretion**: As evidenced by the anonymized track records, the firm values confidentiality and precision over loudness.

## Target Audience
- **International Investors**: Looking for local legal ground-truth in Indonesia.
- **Banking Executives**: Requiring specialized litigation and recovery counsel.
- **Corporate Founders**: Needing strategic advisory for M&A and Pre-IPO.

## Design Tone
- **Atmospheric**: Using dark OLED backgrounds and subtle light leaks to create depth.
- **Editorial**: Focused on powerful typography and intentional negative space.
- **Tactile**: Interactive elements should feel like they have physical weight (spring easing, subtle elevation).

## Anti-References
- Generic "Justice Scale" stock photos.
- Tech-startup "bright blue/purple" gradients.
- Over-animated scroll effects that feel "cheap."
